Patience Aba Sakyi

Patience Aba Sakyi holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Mathematics from the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology, Ghana, obtained in July 2015. She is currently a postgraduate research student with the National Institute for Mathematical Sciences, Ghana, in the 'Scientific Computing and Industrial Modelling' programme. Patience's interests lie in Computational Mathematics, with specific research interest in Data Science, Differential Equations for Forensic Pathology, Computational and Digital Forensics, and Cryptography. Her extracurricular and voluntary community contributions include indulging in community-based Mathematics education and awareness creation, with particular interest in whipping up the interest of girls and young ladies in Mathematics. She hopes to be an integral part of the move to improve and increase national interest, and consequently performance, in Mathematics in Ghana.

Enock Gyan

Enock Gyan is a trained journalist and a communication for development practitioner with a keen interest in using evidence to enhance messaging and external relations for communications to deliver maximum impact. For close to five (5) years he has been working with the media, policymakers and key opinion formers to influence and challenge political, social and economic agendas. His ability to communicate clear, simple and active key messages and research ensures that communications help drive policy change, ultimately leading to better delivery of public services. He has a proven record of ensuring that important information travels where it needs to and that it is heard and understood effectively. He holds a Bachelor of Arts degree in Communication studies from the Ghana Institute of Journalism and currently a postgraduate research student with the Institute of Development and Technology Management in the 'Development Studies-Technology Management option' programme.

FAQ

section1
  • What is Instant Pins?

    Instant Pins is an electronic purchase system which enables the purchase of WAEC exams results and GES school placement access cards etc. across all mobile networks in a few clicks on USSD.

  • What are the benefits of Instant Pins?

    No hidden charges
    Instant delivery of card via SMS or Email
    Available across all mobile networks

  • Who can use Instant Pins?

    Instant Pins is for everyone: any individual with a mobile money wallet across all networks can purchase a card on instant pins

  • What details are required to sign up as a Merchant on Instant Pins?

    Your personal details (Name, phone number, email address and business name)

    • section2
  • Can I purchase a card if I have no money in my mobile money wallet?

    NO

  • What minimum amount do I need in my mobile money wallet to undertake a transaction?

    An amount equivalent to the cost of the particular card you intend to purchase

  • How do I know the cost of the card I intend to purchase?

    Prices are displayed beside each card listed

    • section3
  • Is there any service charge when I purchase a card using Instant Pins?

    NO. There is no service charge.

  • How do I purchase a card using Instant Pins?

    Dial *711*1000#

    Select Individual
    Select the card you want to buy
    Authorize payment from your Mobile Money account using your PIN
    Confirm Payment
    You will receive a notification of a successful payment
    Wait for an SMS with Serial and Pin details of card purchased and URL to access the card

  • What happens if there is a network failure when undertaking a transaction?

    Your transaction status will be pending and either Hubtel or your network provider will send you a message to notify you.

  • What happens if a particular card runs out of stock immediately after successful payment?

    The chances of such an occurrence is very slim. However should this occur you will receive your card immediately the particular card is re-stocked. This should occur within 24 hours, failure of which your money will be refunded back to you.
